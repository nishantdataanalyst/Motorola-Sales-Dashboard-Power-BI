# Motorola-Sales-Dashboard-Power-BI
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyse nationwide sales performance, visualizing key KPIs such as total sales, quantity, transactions, and customer ratings for data-driven decision-making.
This project analyses mobile sales, transactions, quantity trends, customer ratings, payment behavior, and city-wise sales distribution through an interactive Power BI dashboard.
It provides insights on:
Total sales performance (~769M)
Transaction volume (4K+)
Monthly quantity trends
Top sales by city (map visualization)
Brand performance comparison
Payment method share (UPI, debit/credit, cash)
Customer rating distribution
The dashboard helps businesses understand regional demand, brand popularity, seasonal trends, payment preference, and customer satisfaction, enabling data-driven decision making.

 Key Insights Highlighted

✔ August and December show peak unit sales
✔ Apple & Samsung drive the largest sales revenue
✔ UPI and Credit Card are the most used payment modes
✔ Metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai dominate mobile sales
✔ 20.8% customers give a 1-star rating, indicating improvement opportunity.
